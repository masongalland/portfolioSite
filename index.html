About Me

Mason Galland

Web Developer

While working toward my bachelor’s degree in business management at Utah Valley University, I found myself fascinated by the huge impact technology can have on a business. My interest in this area continued to grow during my first job after college working as a website consultant/project manager for the custom website department of a digital marketing company called Boostability. After a year of working alongside web developers, my desire to learn how to build online applications had reached a level that pushed me to further my education by attending an immersive web development program at DevMountain, where I currently work as a student mentor.

My passion for web development grows stronger each day. I love this industry because it challenges me, provides the opportunity to be innovative, and gives me the ability to create without limits. Constantly learning, improving, and challenging myself is important to me, and I excel at everything I put my mind to. Dedication is one my greatest strengths -- I see things to the end and don’t give up.

When I’m not working, I enjoy hiking and biking in the mountains, traveling, reading, and spending time with my family.

Good-Sees

Social Cataloging Application for Movies

Description:

GoodSees was built by a team of 5 in two weeks. It is a social platform that allows its users to search for and browse different movies, then recommend those movies or add them to one of their lists--want-to-see, seen, and favorites. It also allows users to view their friends profiles to see their different lists and what movies they have recommended. The idea stemmed from the website called goodreads.com, which deals with books instead of movies.

Technology Used:

AngularJS, Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL

Date:

March, 2017
